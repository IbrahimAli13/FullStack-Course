Deadpool

Deadpool is a 2016 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. The movie follows the story of Wade Wilson, a former Special Forces operative turned mercenary, who after being subjected to an experimental procedure gains accelerated healing powers. With his newfound abilities, Wilson adopts the alter ego Deadpool and sets out to hunt down the man responsible for his transformation while also seeking revenge on those who wronged him. Known for its irreverent humor, self-awareness, and breaking the fourth wall, Deadpool became a critical and commercial success, popularizing the R-rated superhero genre.
